For those of you reading this who are uninitiated I would like to explain to you the different roles that are in scrum which our group was devided into so that you would have a better idee of what each groupmember learned depending on their role.

Scrum Master
Leads the team in working effectively according to scrum values
Product Owner
Makes sure the scrum team aligns with the product goals
Development Team
Proffesionals responsable for the hands-on work

Of course, there was no product owner for our project since it was a university assignment, however, I guess that one could argue our teachers as being our product owners or the closest thing to it.
Anyhow, we learned alot during these courses and got to feel for ourselves what it meant to build something from scratch on our own.
To me this was some of the most fullfilling times spent during my studies and something to look back on fondly.
