Childhood

His name was lillen because he was tiny. My second pet was a turtle. His name shall not be spoken here. At some point my sister got a parrot which was green.
Surprisingly there are such parrots living in london as well. Who would have guessed, right? Anyhow, i had a pretty ordinary childhood with ups and downs like most other children.
I am, however, thankful for the things that happened since they led me to were i am today.
